One nervous system for every part of your venue operation.

Your staff lives in Telegram, Discord, Slack, and SMS. Your vendors live in email. Your data lives in Quickbooks, your POS, and a dozen spreadsheets. VANDI is the AI layer that connects all of it — so your team coordinates across every channel from one intelligent system.

🚨
Your staff lives in five different apps

Telegram for production, Discord for security, group SMS for vendors, email for management. Information lives in silos. Decisions get made on the wrong thread. Critical updates don't reach the right people in time.

⚠️
Coordination depends on a few people knowing everything

The GM, the production manager, the head of security — three people hold the entire operation in their heads. When one of them is unreachable for fifteen minutes, the whole show wobbles.

📉
Generic software wasn't built for live events

You're using project management tools, spreadsheets, and group texts to run a complex, high-stakes operation. The tools are the problem — not your team.

The real problem

Coordination across too many channels is what's actually killing your operation.

The dream isn't another app. The dream is one intelligent layer that connects to all of them — Telegram, Discord, Slack, email, SMS — and routes the right information to the right person automatically.

VANDI is that layer. Built by an operator, for operators. It speaks every channel your team already uses, so nobody has to switch tools, and nothing falls through the cracks.

How it works

Up and running in three steps.

Your operation is connected, your agents are deployed, and your team starts working from one system. No rip-and-replace. No six-month implementation.

01

Connect your channels

VANDI links into the apps your team already uses — Telegram, Discord, Slack, SMS, email — plus your existing tools (ticketing CSVs, Quickbooks, POS exports). No replacing what works.

02

Deploy your agent network

Six AI agents go live across operations, vendors, compliance, communications, reporting, and risk. Set your parameters. VANDI handles the routing — your team handles the decisions.

03

Run your operation

Coordinate across departments without switching apps. Let agents catch what humans miss. Build a system that gets smarter every event you run.

The platform

Four pillars. One unified system.

VANDI is an AI operating layer for live venues — not an everything-tool. Every capability maps to one of four core domains.

⚙️

Operations

Coordinate the show

Real-time runsheet management, staff coordination, and resource allocation across every department, channel, and shift.

  • Live runsheet builder
  • Staff scheduling & coverage

  • Day-of timeline management

  • Real-time arrival tracking
📁

Compliance

Protect the operation

Track contracts, permits, riders, and insurance certificates with version control and proactive expiration alerts.

  • Contract version control

  • Artist rider review
  • Settlement automation

Intelligence

Live event pacing, post-event debriefs, and historical benchmarking turn raw data into actionable operational insight.

  • Live event pacing dashboard
  • Post-event debrief reports
  • Historical benchmarking
  • Risk monitoring & alerts

The agent network

Six agents.
One unified operation.

Each agent is purpose-built for a specific operational domain. They work together, coordinated by Maestro, so your operation runs without anyone needing to manage the system itself.

ATLAS — Operations Commander

Owns event logistics, runsheets, and resource allocation. Surfaces conflicts before they become incidents.

VEGA — Vendor & Catalog Intelligence

Manages your full vendor network with automated outreach, confirmation, and performance scoring.

FORGE — Document & Compliance

Processes contracts, permits, and riders. Maintains your compliance checklist in real time.

CLIO — Reporting & Analytics

Generates live pacing data, post-event debriefs, and board-ready performance intelligence.

LOKI — Communications & Routing

Routes messages across Telegram, Discord, Slack, SMS, and email — to the right person at the right time.

PYRRHUS — Risk & Incident Intelligence

Runs pre-event risk assessments and maintains your incident response playbook.

Common questions

What you need to know before the call.

Have a question we haven't answered? Reach out directly — we respond within one business day.

VANDI is currently in pilot development. The agent network is functional and the platform is being deployed with our first venue partners. We are not yet generally available, which is exactly why pilot partners get the most favorable terms and the most influence over the roadmap.
Existing systems like Momentus, Prism, and Ungerboeck are built on traditional workflows — forms, fields, and dashboards. VANDI is the first platform built natively on an autonomous AI agent network. Instead of clicking through a UI to manage your event, you have six agents that handle the operational layer for you. The incumbents are tools. VANDI is an operating layer.
VANDI's communications layer connects to Telegram, Discord, Slack, SMS, and email. Your staff doesn't have to learn a new app — they keep using what works for them, and VANDI routes messages, briefings, and updates across all of those channels automatically.
Our agents support operator decisions — they don't replace them. Every consequential decision (financial commitments, safety calls, contract terms) requires human approval. Agents are configured with strict escalation thresholds, and every action is logged with a full audit trail. Liability is governed by our Terms of Service.
Pilot onboarding is typically 10–14 days for an independent venue, longer for multi-venue operators. We need access to your historical event data, vendor list, staff roster, ticketing data exports, and current communication channels. We work directly with your team to configure agents around your specific operation.
Pilot pricing is significantly discounted from list pricing in exchange for case study participation and product feedback. Standard subscription tiers begin at $6,000/year for small independent venues and scale to enterprise contracts for multi-venue operators. Final pricing is structured around your event volume and team size.
Customer data is stored in encrypted databases with role-based access controls. We are pursuing SOC 2 Type II compliance, with that work targeted for completion in our first production release. Detailed security architecture is available under NDA for serious pilot conversations.

Ready to stop coordinating chaos and start running with intelligence?

VANDI is being built with operators, for operators. If you run a venue and want a real conversation about what's possible, the door is open.
